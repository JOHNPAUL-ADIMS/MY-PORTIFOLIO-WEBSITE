Hi, my name is JohnPaul

Ph.D. Student - Supply Chain | Logistics | Transportation

One of the core lessons imprinted on me at a young age was understanding the processes involved in running my father's business. In recent years, I also delved into establishing a supply chain startup, and even though it's no longer in operation, the skills I acquired in the process has engineered my passion for a sustainable supply chain, logistics, and transportation systems.

Presently, I am a Ph.D Student in Industrial Engineering at FAMU-FSU College of Engineering, where Professor Yanshuo Sun advises me. My research mainly lies in the use of application of mathematical modelling, big data, machine learning, Artificial Intelligence, operations research and optimization methods towards improving the planning, operations, management of transportation systems, supply chain and logistics.

My research activites will see my significant contributions to airline operations, supply chain management, public and frieight transportation systems activities.

However, in my free time, I enjoy experiencing, reading and learning new things. I also love to write and speak about them. This is to help people learn too. Therefore, feel free to check out some of them on my blog. I also do love travelling, playing the keyboard, composing music and officiating football matches.
